PDF

For this assignment, I modified a research proposal I wrote before to be an accessible pdf and tested it with the eAccessibility checker.

I followed the instructions to create an accessible pdf as described in the Accessible PDF Author Guide step by step.

Then I uploaded my pdf to the checker and passed most of the tests, with an exception on the inconsistent heading levels.

The problem with that was I don't have a licence to the Adobe Acrobat Pro but a trial version, which caused the software to keep crashing

while I was trying to manually add the headings.

Overall it went pretty good to add accessibility features to the pdf and I do believe a small deed like this could make a big difference

for people with impaired abilities to access the pdf documents
